An Introduction to the Data Set¶

 The data provided was generated by a survey emailed to members of the Women’s Flat Track Derby Association. Roller Derby is a fast-paced contact sport that is primarily played by women. The purpose of the survey was to quantitatively find relations between the sport as serious leisure and sense of identity among its female players.

 Lets import the data and do some fundamental cleaning.

Visualizing Missing Data¶

 The first variables of the following chart, starting from the top down, are the initial data that is automatically generated for each survey. They include the columns from “Response ID” to “Country Code”. Note that these columns contain no missing values. As questions and requests to the surveyee come in, a growth of NaN (missing) values follows. See that the number of non-NaN values shrink as we move further into survey.
